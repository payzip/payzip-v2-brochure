Your official Hockey Wales club payment partner

We know that chasing member payments is time-consuming and doesn’t always result in getting paid. Payzip lets members pay you online and tracks their payments for you, helping you get paid more quickly, more often.
With Payzip you can:
  • Collect every single payment, increasing revenue
  • Know exactly where the club is financially
  • Spend a lot less time on admin
  • Get paid within minutes, not weeks
  • Receive and process payments automatically
  • Spot late payments and instantly chase them up
  • Record all of your payments in one place
You can read more about Payzip on our homepage (opens in a new tab).

With Payzip there are no ongoing costs, limits or subscriptions. The only cost is a small service fee for any card transaction (including Apple Pay / Google Pay), so it's always pay-as-you-go.

As a Hockey Wales club, not only will you enjoy an exclusive discounted rate of 2.95% + £0.35 per card transaction, but Payzip will donate 10% of its service fee (at no cost to clubs) to the Hockey Wales Members' Choice Charity of the Year every time a card payment is made. This year it's mental health charity Platfform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it cost anything to use Payzip?
There are no subscriptions, minimum use periods or limits. For Hockey Wales clubs, the only cost is a discounted service fee of 2.95% + £0.35 per card or mobile payment. This service fee is deducted from the money that the club receives. Cash, cheque, and Bacs payments can be recorded for free. There are no other charges to use Payzip.
Can I see how it works?
Yes! You can book a demo at a time to suit you. We're also planning some group webinars with Hockey Wales - just email hw@payzip.co.uk to be notified of the next webinar.
What member information does Payzip store?
Payzip is built with simplicity and security in mind. A member is simply their name and the email address of the person who should receive their invoices and receipts. That's it! No dates of birth, allergies, next of kin - you already manage that somewhere else.
How does the club get paid?
Payzip combines all cleared card payments into a single transaction, deducts the service fee, and pays this into the club's bank account each week, making reconciliation a breeze. A full breakdown of transactions is provided, of course.
How much is the donation?
It's 10% of the percentage part of our service fee, net of VAT.
How do I get started?
Sign up from this page or the main Payzip home page. Your club registration will be automatically associated with Hockey Wales and the Members' Choice Charity donation scheme.
Where can I learn more?
Our home page has more information about Payzip's benefits and features. You can contact us with any questions you may have, and our knowledgebase is always available with helpful articles and guides too.
