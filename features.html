Payzip features

Dashboard

See key financial information at-a-glance. Overdue invoices are surfaced for your attention too.

Invoice management

Easily send invoices to individuals or groups of members. Add timestamped notes to an invoice. See an audit trail of significant events.

Member opt-in payments

Pings let your members respond and pay for things they're interested in - ideal for tour deposits, kit orders, summer camp, and much more.

Automatic payment reminders

Avoid awkward conversations with automatic unpaid invoice reminders. You can exclude specific members from receiving reminders too.

Mobile payments

Apple Pay & Google Pay supported, along with UK debit and credit cards.

Automatic receipts

Every payment gets an automatic email receipt, no matter the payment method.

Automated payouts

Card payments are grouped into a single weekly payout and paid to the club in one transaction. Full remittance report provided.

Record manual payments

Record cash, cheque, and Bacs payments alongside automatically-recorded card and mobile payments.

Email delivery tracking

Be alerted immediately when a member's email address is incorrect. Quickly correct it and resend outstanding invoices.

Multiple users

Add additional managers to your team to share the load - it's free!

Member management

Import, group and edit members as you need them. It's easy to bulk-move members to a new group too.

Export to CSV

Filter any onscreen table how you want it, then export exactly what you see to a CSV file for use elsewhere.
