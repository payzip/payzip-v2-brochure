Faster for you

As a volunteer, your time is precious. Online payments, automatic receipts, easy payment reminders, and simplified payouts all help you get your head out of the books and back into the game.

Convenient for members

Let them pay, their way. Save time at training or rehearsal - members can pay at home using their favourite device and preferred payment method.

Safer for everyone

Reduce in-person contact by asking members to pay the club online. No cash, no germs, no problem.
With Payzip you can...

Take mobile and card payments

Flexible payment options to suit how your club works. Let members pay by:
  • Apple Pay / Google Pay
  • UK credit or debit card
  • bank transfer
  • cheque
  • cash

See your club's finances at-a-glance

Instantly understand the club’s financial position. The dashboard shows you how much is overdue and owed, what has been collected, what’s due in the next payout, and how much is still processing.

Overdue invoices are automatically surfaced for your attention, making chasing payments a breeze (more on that in a moment).

Card payments are grouped into one simple payout per week, with a full breakdown of the member payments included.

Avoid awkward conversations

For those times where a gentle reminder is needed, Payzip lets you send unpaid invoice reminders by email, to those who have overdue invoices or just an amount outstanding. You control the groups or individual members who receive them.

You can also exclude a member’s invoice from receiving reminders, and make notes on the invoice too - useful for handling sensitive situations, or simply keeping others on the management team up-to-date.

Save time with automatic receipts

No matter how a member pays, they’ll get a receipt by email automatically when the payment is recorded - saving you time, and reassuring them their money has been received.

Even better, every club member can see their current invoices and past payments on their own dashboard when they create a Payzip account (optional).
