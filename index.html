With Payzip you can...

Invoice your members

Quickly create an invoice for your whole club, a group, or a single member. Members can pay by card, bank transfer, cheque or cash.

Track payments

Always see up-to-date information on who has paid and, more importantly, who still owes money to the club. Receive one simple payout per week, with a full report of member payments.

Send reminders

Send invoice reminders to everyone who owes money, or just those who are overdue. Filter by group or individual member.

Stay in control

Exclude members from receiving payment reminders, add a note to any invoice. Ideal for team organisers to keep track of the conversation around payment.
Features

Simple administration

Quickly create invoices and have members pay them by card online. See owed and overdue amounts instantly - Payzip surfaces overdue invoices for your attention and action. Send payment reminders. Record cash and cheque payments.

Payments, by the numbers

Gain deep insights into your club’s financial performance, including the average time-to-pay of your whole club, a group, or a single member.

Pay, your way

With Apple Pay and Google Pay. Members can pay in seconds using a glance or the touch of a button. UK credit and debit cards also supported. Direct Debit support coming soon.

Group, tag, sort

Arrange your members in a way that makes most sense to you and your team.
